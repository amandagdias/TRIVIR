1 Motivation and Goals
In order to analyze the development, influence and application of experience during
problem solving and to draw conclusions for the development of Case-Based
Reasoning systems [Ko93, RiSch89] we observed and videotaped test persons while
they were solving simple design problems with the computer program “The
Incredible Machine”*. The video and audio recordings were protocolled and analyzed.
