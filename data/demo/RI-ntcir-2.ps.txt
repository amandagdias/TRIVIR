Abstract
This paper describes our retrieval system for
NTCIR­2 Japanese/English CLIR and MLIR tasks. We
integrate query and document translation with mono­lingual 
retrieval to improve retrieval accuracy, and
perform clustering to improve browsing efficiency. We
also introduce an entropy­driven technique in evaluat­ing 
clustering methods.
